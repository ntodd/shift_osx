 The Console at the bottom of Shift lists all of the actions you made in the current session accompanied by a timestamp. It can be shown or hidden using the toolbar icon at the top that says "Show/Hide Console". You can also clear the console by using the "Clear Console" button. If those two icons are not located in your toolbar then right-click in the toolbar area and select "Customize Toolbar..." from the menu. You should then see a window of icons which you can add to your Shift toolbar including the two mentioned above.
